About us

Finding and excelling within a niche is a rare and valuable gift. It means knowing where you're meant to play and leaning on others to support when neccesary. What's more powerful is finding partners who amplufy your talents and for whom you do the same. That's the story of One Studio.

One Studios origins where a natural progression after several years of collaboration between three specialized digital companies - FONK Cape Town, AUX Studio (branding and ux design), and Flatrock Solutions (Computer software and enterprise software development). Initially we began collaborating to support each other in specialized aspects in different digital projects. Having built deep trust and a genuine admiration of each other's areas of expertise, over time we noticed that a genuine gap existed which prevented entrepreneurs and companies looking for digital solutions to have a single destination to support their needs across the digital lifecycle.

After many coffees and realization that we shared deeper values and a common picture of the future we decided to formalize our relationship to better deliver value to our clients wherever they are in their digital journeys. Although we began as three individual specialized teams we evolved into One Studio and in the process we have learned so much from each other, grown with each other, and delivered great results for our partners and their products.

What we do

Our approach

No clients, only Partners. Every project we take on we treat as if we're part of the production team--for us , everything we do is personal. Our team, our collective expertise, and our ability to deliver repeated results at pace are worth their weight in gold.

Building and supporting digital products that perform well and gain traction is only half the battleof the product development. At One Studio we treat the support and capacity building of our partners with the same degree of importance as quality design and engineering.

Our in-house team has deep expertise in 3 core areas - Design, Engineering and Software Development, and Growth and Digital Marketing. More specifically we excel at digital product innovation and new product development, developing scalable, enterprise ready custom software, creatively designing and refining brands and digital products that deliver value to users, and using online marketing techniques (SEM) to grow users, revenue, and / or retention.
